A Prophecy Foretold

A musical force foretold in a palmreading by cast's grandmother, The Shaggs were an outsider art ensemble the likes of which modern society has never seen since.

Works of Art

Heaven Sent Talents

  • Hacked-At Drumbeats
  • Whacked-Around Chords
  • Songs with little to no meter.
  • Creating dissonance and beauty, chaos and tranquility.
  • Expanding listener's pre-existing notions of relationship between talent, originality, and ability.

Worlds Seen

Born, raised, and currently flourishing in the great state of New Hampshire.

Collaborations

While never formally working with any other artists, their work inspired a full tribute album featuring the talents of Ida, Optiganally Yours, R. Stevie Moore, Deerhoof and Danielson Famille.

Highest Praise

Kurt Cobain ranked Philosophy of the World No. 5 on his 50 best albums list.
