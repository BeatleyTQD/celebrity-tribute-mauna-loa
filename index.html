About

Helen Wiggins, the oldest sister of The Shaggs was born in Kitty, Maine in 1946. Dorothy and Betty Wiggins were born in Portmouth, Newhampshire in 1948 and 1950.

Known As

As a group they were known as The Shaggs. Dorothy Wiggins was the only sister who had a recognizeable nickname and often reffered to as "Dot". Helen and Betty went by their regular names.

(Unknown) Collaborations

Joy Gregory and Gunnar Madsen both collaborated with The Shaggs for their album Philosophy of The World
