Life Outside The Limelight

Dorothy 'Dot' Wiggin-Semprini

Dorothy was born March 24th, 1948 in Portsmouth, New Hampshire. She sang and played guitar in the band. She married Fred Semprini and had two children, William and Matthew. Dot went onto clean houses for a living before returning to the stage to perform as the leader of the "Dot Wiggin Band". She currently resides in Fremont, New Hampshire.

Helen Wiggin-Bickford

Helen was born December 17th, 1946 in Exeter, New Hampshire. She played the drums in the band. She married her first boyfriend, Henry Bickford. She worked briefly as a dietary aide at a nursing home before sadly passing away in 2006 at the age of 59.

Betty Wiggin-Porter

Betty was born December 24th 1950, in Kittery, Maine. She sang and played the guitar. She married Ricky Porter and had two sons. Betty moved on to work in the stockroom of a kitchen goods warehouse. She currently resides in Fremont, New Hampshire.

Rachel Wiggin-Gould (not pictured)

Rachel was born in 1953, she joined the band occasionaly on base guitar. She would later obtain a business degree from Northern Maine Community College. She now works at Gould Country Candles, a business she started with her husband, Ronald Gould Sr. They currently reside in East Wakefield, New Hampshire.

The Man Behind The Curtain

Austin Wiggin

The Shaggs were formed at the insistence of their controlling father, Austin Wiggin Jr. He passed away of a massive heartattack in 1975, when he was just 47 years old. Following his death the band split. His widow, Annie Wiggin, died in 2005.
